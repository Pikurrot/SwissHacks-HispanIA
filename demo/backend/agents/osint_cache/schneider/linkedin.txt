About:
Hubertus Schneider is the owner and CEO of Schneider Automotive Supply AG, a Zug-based Tier-1 supplier of precision body and chassis components to European OEMs including BMW, Stellantis, and Volkswagen Group. The company employs over 400 people across three Swiss facilities and has invested heavily in factory automation over the past five years. Hubertus is a hands-on industrial operator with a strong capital discipline — he manages a global balanced investment portfolio structured to sit alongside, not compete with, his private corporate capital. His wife Carmen is active in Zurich arts sponsorship and local cultural philanthropy. He has two children, including his daughter Chloe.

Experience:
- CEO & Owner, Schneider Automotive Supply AG (2008–present) — Tier-1 auto component supplier. BMW, Stellantis, VW Group accounts.
- Board Member, Swiss Automotive Suppliers Association (AGVS) (2015–present)
- VP Operations, Keller Fertigungstechnik AG (2003–2008) — industrial automation

About:
Julian Ammann is the founder and public face of the Ammann Retail Group, a prominent Swiss national retail brand known for its commitment to transparent sourcing and local integrity. Julian built the brand from a single store in Zurich in 2004 into a network of 38 locations across German and French Switzerland. He is exceptionally analytical — applying consumer data, logistics metrics, and supply chain governance frameworks to every operational and investment decision. He is a regular keynote speaker on corporate ethics and ESG supply chain practices at Swiss and European forums. Julian is also an active art collector with a focus on emerging Swiss artists.

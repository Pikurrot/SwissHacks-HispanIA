About:
Marius Huber is a Swiss-based investor and philanthropist focused exclusively on biodiversity and ecological restoration. After a successful exit from GreenHarvest AG — a sustainable agriculture company he co-founded in 2015 — Marius transitioned to full-time impact stewardship. He serves on the advisory board of the Amazon Biodiversity Foundation and co-chairs the Supply Chain Transparency Working Group at Swiss Sustainable Finance. His wife Elena leads the foundation's educational youth programme on ecological finance.

Experience:
- Advisory Board Member at Amazon Biodiversity Foundation (2022–present)
- Co-Chair, Supply Chain Transparency WG at Swiss Sustainable Finance (2023–present)
- Co-Founder & CEO at GreenHarvest AG (2015–2022) — exit via acquisition. Sustainable agriculture and regenerative farming.

Name: Eugen Räber
Headline: Retired Founder & CEO, Räber Präzisionstechnik AG | Trustee | Private Investor
Location: Küsnacht, Zurich

About:
Eugen Räber founded Räber Präzisionstechnik AG in 1981 — a Zurich-based precision engineering firm supplying components to Swiss watchmaking and medical device industries. He sold the company in 2016 after a 35-year tenure and now focuses on conservative wealth stewardship and family philanthropy. He serves as trustee for a private family foundation supporting education and local cultural institutions in the Küsnacht area. Eugen is a member of the Rotary Club Küsnacht and maintains a deliberately minimal online presence. His investment approach mirrors his engineering background: conservative, methodical, built for longevity.
